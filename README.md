**Overview**
This is a plugin for Google Chrome which will provide a definition for any term you find on the internet, and will contexualize the word in your specified field of interest. For example, if I am a "neuroscientist interested in GPCR signaling", and add "MAP Kinase" to my library, the plugin will generate an overview of MAP Kinase as it relates to my interests in GPCR signaling. Included in each term are some reference papers which will tie together the newly added term and your field of interest. 

**Installation**
To install and use this plugin, follow these instructions. 

First - download this repository. 

Second - open Google Chrome and go to chrome://extensions

Third - toggle "Developer Mode" and click "Load Unpacked".

Fourth - upload the downloaded folder. Congrats! The plugin should now appear on your browser. 

Fifth - Finally, click on the plugin and enter in your field of interest, followed by a Claude API key. 

**Usage**
To use this extension in Chrome, simply highlight a word or phrase which you'd like to learn more about. Then, right click and press "Add to term library". Wait a couple of minutes for the term to appear in your library, complete with field-relevant information and articles.
